Leap Motion

What is Leap Motion?

Leap Motion is a Kinect-like motion-sensor that uses infrared LEDs and cameras to track the motion of your hands. At almost 300 frames per second, it can accurately track all 10 digits, including joints, in 3D space.

Our Mission

With Project LEAP, we hope to ease the process of rehabilitation through a fun and interactive platform which incorporates the movement of the fingers. We will be tapping on the capabilities of the Leap Motion sensor to create a game which suits the needs of our target audience.
